WHAT WE DO

IML

IN-MOLD LABELING

Embee is known for its high-quality in-mold labeling process. We provide IML consumer packaging for food and personal care, household, and cosmetic brands. We prepare the mold to ensure the label merges with the packaging to create a single product. It is part of a pre-printed decorative film placed in the mold to create the desired shape. These shapes and sizes are customizable and designed to the client’s choice.

KEY PRODUCT FEATURES

An important aspect of Embee Flexilable, allows clients to meet their production and distribution deadlines while ensuring that their products are properly labeled and packaged to meet industry standards.

HIGHLY ECONOMICAL

Our IML production cost is economical, and we provide affordable charges for the client, making us cost-efficient compared to our competitors.

Durability

The finishing is clean and smooth as part of the integrated IML process for packaging our client’s products. The labeling and flexible packaging is tamper-proof and works well in humid and extreme temperature conditions. We have used high-impact and scratch-resistant material that fuses permanently onto the container.

Eco-Friendly

We use a recyclable plastic resin that helps decrease carbon footprint. Embee Flexilabel has ensured all our IML products are made from eco-friendly materials.

Design Quality

We use high-resolution colors for the images to stand out on the brand labels with both side displays. We also can provide the customer with enhanced graphic effects enabling unique design ideas to be highlighted in the packaging.

With a focus on state-of-the-art technology and customization, we ensure that each of our clients receives the best solution for their specific needs. Trust us to be your partner in achieving your labeling and flexible packaging goals.
