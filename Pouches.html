KEY PRODUCT FEATURES

Clients approach us with anticipation as we efficiently digitally print for any batch size, from small orders to bulk. We have quick turnaround times, enabling our clients to rely on us for the best quality and service.

Plastic Barrier film

Plastic barrier films are an essential component of stand-up pouches. We design them with a plastic barrier to protect the product from moisture, oxygen, and other contaminants affecting its quality and shelf life.

Solvent free lamination

We use solvent-free lamination is a process used to bond different layers of plastic barrier films together without the use of harmful solvents, ensuring the safety of the product and the environment.

Hang holes

Our special hand-hole feature is designed to allow customers to carry the stand-up pouch more comfortably. Hang holes are added to the top of the pouch and are tear-resistant, ensuring it is strong enough to support the weight of the product.

High resistance

We make high-resistance pouches making them last longer. They are designed to be strong, durable, and resistant to tearing, puncturing, or other forms of damage that can occur during transportation or storage.

Finish options

We have innumerable finish options which be customized for each product mix. We enable detailing to enhance the appearance of the stand-up pouches, which can be matte or glossy, depending on the desired aesthetic, and can add an extra layer of protection to the plastic barrier film.

Tear notches

We have designed tear notches accurately in size and added them at the top of the stand-up pouch, making it easier for customers to open the package. The customized design enables it to be torn cleanly and precisely without affecting the integrity of the packaging or the quality of the product inside.

Super quality zip lock

Our new age superior quality zip lock pouches provide a resealable closure that allows customers to open and close the packaging multiple times, ensuring the product stays fresh for longer. They are easy to use, durable, and leak-proof, preventing air, moisture, or other contaminants from entering the pouch and affecting the product.

With a focus on state-of-the-art technology and customization, we ensure that each of our clients receives the best solution for their specific needs. Trust us to be your partner in achieving your labeling and flexible packaging goals.
