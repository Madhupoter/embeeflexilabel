Moisture sealing

Our use of high-quality materials for packing ensures complete coverage sealing it away from any kind of contaminants that would lurk from outside and during handling. Ensuring the pack is airtight and moisture sealed at all times.

Solvent free lamination

We use solvent-free lamination is a process used to bond different layers of plastic barrier films together, adhering to food-grade standards. These laminates have high bonding strengths between the substrates of the pack.
